# USE CASE: 5 Add a New Employee's Details

## CHARACTERISTIC INFORMATION

### Goal in Context

As an *HR advisor* I want *to add a new employee's details* so that *I can ensure the new employee is paid.*

### Scope

Company.

### Level

Primary task.

### Preconditions

HR advisor has received all required employee information. HR system is accessible.

### Success End Condition

The new employee’s details are added to the system and are available for payroll.

### Failed End Condition

The new employee’s details are not added and they cannot be paid.

### Primary Actor

HR Advisor.

### Trigger

A new employee joins the organisation.

## MAIN SUCCESS SCENARIO

1. HR advisor receives required documentation for the new employee.
2. HR advisor logs into the HR system.
3. HR advisor enters the employee’s personal, job, and salary details.
4. HR advisor submits and confirms the entry.
5. The employee’s details are saved and available to payroll.

## EXTENSIONS

3. **Incomplete or incorrect documentation**:
    1. HR advisor contacts relevant parties to obtain or correct missing information.

## SUB-VARIATIONS

None.
